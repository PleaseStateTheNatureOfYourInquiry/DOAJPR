Diamond Open Access Journal for Planetary Research Initiative
=============================================================


Welcome to the Diamond Open Access Journal for Planetary Research initiative. This is a placeholder website for the initiative.

If you are interested in participating and contributing to the discussion and actions, then please consider joining at the dedicated
`MatterMost <https://mattermost.solarsystem.social/signup_user_complete/?id=7n5zgnshu38fmxnmw7bn8654xa&md=link&sbr=fa>`_ channel.

There is a monthly online meeting, the (links to the) recordings of which can be found in the :ref:`meetings section <meetings>`.
